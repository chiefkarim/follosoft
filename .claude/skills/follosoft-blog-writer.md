# FolloSOFT Blog Writer

## Purpose

This skill generates practical, benefit-driven blog posts targeted at snow removal business owners and operators. The content must be helpful, easy to read, and match FolloSOFT’s professional yet approachable tone.

## Target Audience

Busy snow removal contractors (often not highly tech-savvy). Use simple language, avoid heavy jargon, and focus on real-world time/money/safety benefits.

## Tone & Style Guidelines

- **Tone**: Professional, helpful, solution-oriented, like an experienced advisor.
- **Voice**: Direct “you” language (“You can save hours by…”).
- **Style**: Actionable, checklist-heavy, benefit-focused. Short paragraphs, scannable sections.
- **Length**: Aim for 800–1200 words (8–12 minute read time).
- **Title Style**: Benefit-oriented main title + colon + specific promise  
  Example: “Snow Plow Maintenance Guide: Keep Your Fleet Running All Winter”

## Required Structure for Every Blog Post

```markdown
# [Benefit-Driven Title]

[Short introduction: State the problem + what the reader will learn/gain]

## [H2 Section 1]

[Content with short paragraphs, bullets, or numbered lists]

## [H2 Section 2]

...

## [Final Section – often a checklist or key takeaways]

## Conclusion

[Summary of main benefits + soft call-to-action toward using better tools or FolloSOFT]

Ready to take your snow removal business to the next level? Book a demo with FolloSOFT.
```

## Workflow (Always Follow This Order)

1. **Clarify** – If needed, ask for topic, target length, or specific angle.
2. **Research** – Use known information about snow removal best practices and FolloSOFT features.
3. **Create Outline** – Build clear H2 sections with benefit focus.
4. **Write Draft** – Follow the structure above.
5. **Optimize** – Add checklists, numbered steps, bold key benefits.
6. **Review** – Ensure it’s practical, scannable, and matches FolloSOFT tone.
7. **Output** – Deliver clean Markdown + suggested reading time and meta description (optional).

## Examples of Good Titles (Match This Pattern)

- Snow Plow Route Optimization: Maximize Efficiency with Smart Routing
- Post-Winter Maintenance Guide for Snow Removal Equipment
- Snow Removal Safety Training: Protecting Your Team
- How Snow Removal Software Transforms Business Operations

## What to Include / Avoid

**Include:**

- Real-world practical tips
- Numbered lists and checklists
- Clear benefits (time saved, money saved, reduced risk)
- Subtle, natural mentions of software benefits when relevant

**Avoid:**

- Heavy sales language
- Overly technical jargon without explanation
- Long walls of text
- First-person “I” stories unless requested

## When to Use This Skill

Use `follosoft-blog-writer` whenever the user says:

- “Write a blog post about…”
- “Create a FolloSOFT-style article on…”
- “Generate content for snow removal blog…”
